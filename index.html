CrossSell Builder 2.0

In the world of tomorrow, you never have to look up skus to build a cross sell. You just save a description of what you want to go in each slot. SOLR, the search engine that powers search on our website, is then responsible for finding the best item to match your description. The best part is, if the best match sells out, SOLR will find the next closest item and automatically put it into the crosssell.
